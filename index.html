    • About the Sun

    The Sun is a G2V spectral type star located at the center of the solar system. It's a sphere of plasma composed mostly of hydrogen and helium, emitting light and heat due to nuclear reactions in its core. It's the primary source of energy for life on Earth and has a mass approximately 333,000 times that of Earth.
