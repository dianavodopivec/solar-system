SUN

The Sun, our Solar System's central star, provides light and energy essential for life on Earth through nuclear fusion. It displays phenomena like sunspots and solar flares, while also stabilizing the solar system through its gravitational pull. Additionally, the Sun's lifecycle, from formation to eventual evolution into a red giant and white dwarf, offers valuable insights into stellar evolution and the origins of life in the universe.
